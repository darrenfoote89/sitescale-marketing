I wanted to share a tool I think you'll find genuinely useful. SiteScale lets you load a satellite map of any address, draw your site plan over it, take real measurements, and generate a professional quote PDF — all without installing anything. It runs entirely in your browser and works on your phone.

What sets it apart: you're not locked into any particular map. If you have a drone photo, county parcel map, or downloaded aerial, upload it directly and calibrate to scale from any known measurement on the image. It works with whatever you're already pulling on a job.
